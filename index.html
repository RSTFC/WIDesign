Company Profile


ABOUT US

In 2015 Wayne Ebden started collecting pallets and made different kinds of furniture for people on request. From there he decided to start a small business from home and continues to flourish through the years, with the company offering it's products from the largest industries to the smallest niche markets.

His passion lies in creating pieces of art from wood and to make every home and business magical.


OUR VALUES

WI DESIGN strive to deliver best-in-class carpentry, contracting services to clients and large businesses. In order to achieve this, we hold ourselves to some pretty big ideas. These values run through 100% of our operations and we aim to excel at:
